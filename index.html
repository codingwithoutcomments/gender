Helpline

I am a young queer person and I’m having a personal emergency, and I don’t have anyone else to talk to about it. I need help.

While I am absolutely blown away by your trust and faith in me, I’m not a licensed shrink or counselor. If you are in a crisis, please call The Trevor Project’s free hotline at 866 488 7386. Those peeps are great.
